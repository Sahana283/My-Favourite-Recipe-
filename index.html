Ingredients needed:

  • Readymade Pie Crust(Baked)
  • Chocolate ganache
  • Whipped Cream
  • Chocolate Chips

To make your chocolate pie:

  1. Make sure your readymade pie crust is freshly baked and warm.
  2. Pour in your creamy chocolate ganache into the pie and keep it in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours.
  3. Top it off with yummy whipped cream and chocolate chips.
  4. Your pie is ready to eat! Best served when chilled.
